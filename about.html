About

At Beauty By Brittany, I am dedicated to making you look and feel amazing. I became a fully qualified makeup artist at 17, holding my certificate III in makeup and a diploma of screen and media. I have studied at CIT and the Canberra Makeup Academy. I offer a variety of services, and use proven techniques and high end products.

My clients become my family, and I look forward to painting all your beautiful faces. For more information and to schedule a session, contact me today.
